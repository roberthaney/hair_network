OUR STYLISTS

Valerie

In 1992 Valerie began her own business, Hair Network because she thought, like so many business owners think; I can do this better myself. In particular what she was most interested in doing was cultivating a client base of like-minded people. Folks who needed a stylist who was compelled to listen carefully to their thoughts regarding their relationship with their hair. Someone who was interested in thinking out of the box or staying within the confines of a clients' comfort zone. A hairdresser who could make suggestions, be creative as well as thoughtful about cutting and coloring hair.

Ashley

Ashley's career as a stylist began in 2007. Her creative energy paired with her love for people, make her passionate about her career. Visiting hair shows, classes and hands on education inspire her to keep up with trends and innovative techniques. Ashley's goal is to listen to her client's needs and create a style they can replicate and maintain at home. Ideally building a relationship with her clients, and making each individual feel great in their own skin!
